Sum square difference

The sum of the squares of the first ten natural numbers is, $$ 1^2+2^2+\ldots+1-^2=385 $$ The square of the sum of the first ten natural numbers is, $$ (1 + 2 + ... + 10)^2 = 55^2 = 3025 $$ Hence the difference between the sum of the squares of the first ten natural numbers and the square of the sum is 3025 − 385 = 2640.
